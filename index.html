My research

I am an aerospace engineer, currently in the last year of PhD at Politecnico di Torino, Italy.
My research topic is artificial intelligence for mission autonomy, focusing on payload data selection, event recognition and failure detection.
System engineering background, in my university years I participated on two CubeSats mission that we developed and launched.






Neural Networks

Algorithms for event recognition and image prioritization for an Interplanetary CubeSat Mission.

Failure Detection

Fuzzy logics algorithms for detecting actuator failures in small satellites. Implementations in Matlab / Simulink.

Tradespace Exploration

Genetic algorithms to optimize the tradespace exploration during the pre-phase A (phase zero) of a small satellite mission.
